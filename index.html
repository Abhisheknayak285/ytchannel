About Me

Hey everyone, I'm Rinku Nayak, and I embarked on this YouTube adventure because I have a deep passion for sharing the latest updates on major infrastructure developments in India. My journey started in 2022, and since then, I’ve been committed to providing you with reliable and exciting information about projects like the Delhi-Mumbai Expressway, Bullet Train, Metro systems, and other ongoing construction updates.

Being from Surat, Gujarat, I’m truly excited to share my knowledge and keep you informed about these groundbreaking projects that are shaping the future of our country. My channel will focus on in-depth updates, analyses, and discussions around these developments.

I love connecting with my viewers, so don’t hesitate to leave your thoughts, suggestions, or comments—I’m always here to interact with you. Thank you for joining me on this incredible journey, and I look forward to growing with all of you!
